Who we are

The Community of Data Science is a youth run organisation/ student run initiative, where you can expect to:-

  • LEARN about the latest technologies and topics you are passionate about.
  • DEVELOP your technical, communication, inter-personal and leadership skills.
  • GROW into a confident, knowledgeable and technically informed individual.

This community is all about bringing together dedicated and zealous students in all ﬁelds of Technology. At C.O.D.S. we aim to interweave learning and fun through interactive sessions and to engage members of the community and convey information in an informal environment. From discussions to hands-on sessions to workshops, we bring together everything that’s required to improve an understanding of essesntial technolgies in the current world.

F.A.Q

Is This Open For University Students?

Yes! We are a youth-run community predominantly for university students.

My university doesn't have this community. Can I still be a member?

Definitely! You can be a chapter lead and build the community within your campus.

I am not in Computer Science. Can I still be a part of it?

Of Course! We have no bar on what degree you are pursuing. Any university student can be a part of our community.

Are there any fees to be a part of the community?

None at all!

I don't know much about the domains of C.O.D.S. Can I still be a part of it?

Yes, of course! C.O.D.S focuses on building the technical and interpersonal skills from the ground up.

What's the difference between a chapter lead and a community member?

Anyone can be our community member. Chapter leaders head the community in their respective universities. Their aim is to bring together the community within their campus.

What exactly happens in C.O.D.S?

Our community focuses on frequent community gatherings within their campus to help hone their skills in the domains. Other miscellaneous activities occur, such as workshops and cross-university sessions.

My question isn't here. How do I contact you?

For other questions, you can reach out to us on the mail ID in the contact section.
